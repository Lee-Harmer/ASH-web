Our Story

In-Tents About What We Do

A pun, a passion, and a promise to make every event extraordinary.

How It Started

Born From a Love of Light and Gathering

In-Tents Lighting began with a simple observation: the most memorable events aren't defined by their size, their budget, or even their venue — they're defined by the feeling in the air. The warmth of a string light caught in someone's eye. The way a bedouin tent turns an ordinary field into something intimate and magical.

We set out to bottle that feeling and make it available to everyone. Whether you're planning a 10-person bridal shower or a 200-person wedding reception, we believe you deserve a space that takes your breath away.

The name? Yes, it's a pun. We're in-tents about what we do — intensely passionate, deeply committed, and always a little bit excited about what we get to create next.

Our Approach

Structure First, Magic Second

Great events don't happen by accident. They happen because someone cared about every detail — from where the tables sit to how the light falls at golden hour.

We start every event with a brief, a site visit, and a very strong cup of coffee. We listen. We plan. We source, set up, and style everything ourselves — so on the day, all you have to do is arrive and enjoy.
