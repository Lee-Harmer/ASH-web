Bridal Showers

Because She Only Gets One Last Hurrah

A bridal shower is about more than gifts and games — it's about gathering the women (and people) she loves most, in a space that feels warm, joyful, and completely her.

We create outdoor celebrations full of personality: gorgeous grazing boards, themed styling, shaded canopies, and all the little touches that make every guest feel like they're exactly where they're supposed to be.

What We Bring

Everything for a Perfect Celebration

Shaded Canopy or Tent

A beautiful canopy, stretch tent, or parasol setup that keeps your guests cool and comfortable while looking gorgeous in every photo.

Grazing & Styling Tables

Farm tables or trestle setups styled to perfection — the perfect base for a spectacular grazing board or themed display.

Seating Arrangements

Cross-back chairs, picnic cushions, or a mix of both — configured for your guest count and outdoor space.

Themed Décor

From boho botanicals to glam gold-and-blush — we style every element to match your chosen aesthetic and the bride-to-be's personality.

Signage & Personal Touches

Welcome signs, name tags, activity stations, and sweet little details that make guests feel celebrated and cared for.

Full Setup & Pack-Down

We handle everything before the guests arrive and after they leave — so the host can be present for every single moment.
