Common Questions

Before You Get in Touch

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and large events, 3–6 months is ideal. For parties and smaller events, a few weeks is usually sufficient — but reach out early to avoid disappointment.

Do you travel outside your area?

Yes. We travel to your venue — wherever that is. Travel fees may apply for distant locations. Let us know your venue and we'll confirm.

Can I rent individual items without full event planning?

Absolutely. You can rent tents, furniture, lighting, or décor items individually. Request a quote on the Rentals page or reach out directly.

What's included in setup?

We handle delivery, full setup, styling, and pack-down. Unless agreed otherwise, everything is in place before your guests arrive and gone before you wake up the next morning.
