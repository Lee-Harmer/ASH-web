Our Approach to Weddings

The Day You've Always Imagined

A wedding is not just a party — it's the beginning of a story. The space where it unfolds should feel like it was built just for you: intimate, luminous, and utterly unforgettable.

We work with couples across South Africa to design and deliver wedding environments that reflect their unique vision. From sprawling marquee receptions lit by a thousand string lights to intimate garden ceremonies with hand-styled tablescapes — we bring it all together, seamlessly.

What's Included

Everything for the Perfect Setting

Marquee & Tent Structures

Beautiful white stretch tents, bedouin canopies, and tipi marquees that transform any outdoor venue into a sheltered, elegant reception space.

Ambient Lighting

Festoon lights, fairy light curtains, Edison bulbs, chandeliers, and uplighting — the golden glow that makes every photograph magical.

Farm Tables & Chairs

Rustic wooden farm tables, cross-back chairs, bench seating, and elegant rounds — configured for any layout or guest count.

Table Styling & Décor

Table runners, charger plates, candle holders, lanterns, and botanical elements — every table styled as a composition in its own right.

Setup & Pack-Down

We arrive early, set everything with precision, and return after the event to pack down — so you can enjoy every moment without logistical worry.

Custom Consultation

A dedicated planning session to understand your vision, tour the venue, and create a layout that brings your wedding aesthetic to life.
