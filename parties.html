Birthday Parties

Because Age is Just an Excuse to Party

We've set up birthday parties that range from intimate 20-person dinners under string lights to sprawling 150-person festival-style outdoor events. No matter the size, the approach is always the same: make it feel special, personal, and alive.

Activity stations, themed tablescapes, designated food and drink areas, mood lighting — we design every zone so that guests know exactly where to go, what to do, and how to have the best night of the year.
